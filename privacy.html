Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Data Collection

Bazaar E-Network operates as a decentralized DAO. We do not collect names, emails, or phone numbers. Your identity is represented solely by your Pi Network Public Wallet Address.

2. Blockchain Transparency

Transaction data, including Security Bonds and Trust Scores, are recorded on the Pi Blockchain. This data is public by design to ensure merchant accountability.

3. Security

We never ask for your Passphrase. Security is managed via the Pi Browser's secure authentication layer.
